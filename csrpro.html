Corporate Social Responsibility Projects

For the people that shape our future

We provide free training services to selected fresh graduates that have shown excellence in their academics, dedication to their career path and compassion for their community.

For the people who want love and equality

We provide free training services to selected companies every Pride Month to advocate for policies that will protect everyone's dignity, no matter who they love or who they are.

For the beautiful planet we live in

We provide free training services to selected companies every Earth Day to share the best corporate practices and creating more benefits for communities while cutting carbon.
