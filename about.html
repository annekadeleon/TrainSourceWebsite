About TrainSource

Who We Are

TrainSource specialises in helping our clients identify people and process challenges that may be preventing their organisations from obtaining the best possible results. We take a collaborative approach to create a tailored strategy for success. We adopt a synergistic approach to create a custom fit strategy for success. TrainSource's goal is to partner with companies big and small to evaluate, visualise, create and implement compelling training that improves productivity and performance, as well as influences unceasing concentration on quality through an extensive spectrum of learning solutions.

TrainSource provides learning and talent development solutions with over 25 years of experience in procedure, technology and performance advancement serving clients throughout the world. We are focused on creating lifelong relationships with our clients and providing world-class customer service and customer service and customised training programmes and solutions. We listen, respons and act to enable you to achieve the changes you seek to achieve your targets.

Our Core Values

Integrity

Integrity is at the heart of our individual and corporate actions. Our unwavering belief in integrity is what allows our customers to entrust their business to us. We will ensure honesty and fairness in all our actions. We will always do the right thing.

Respect

Respect towards our clients and consultants guide us in all our decisions. We honour the dignity of all people and respect every client we work with. We place great importance to being honest and forthright in everything we do while understanding and valuing the contributions and priorities of others.

Teamwork

We believe teamwork empowers our individual strengths. Our team exists to support each other in creating a superior work environment and delivering an exceptional customer experience. It is important for us to cooperate and work together for the common good of our clients and our company's mission.

Commitment

We make sure to continuously pursue our goals, especially when experiencing adversity. We care about building long-term relationships with our clients and providing world-class customer service. We will commit to giving our best in everything we do.
